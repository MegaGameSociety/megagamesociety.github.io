About The MegaGame Society

We're a collection of game designers, graphic artists, and megagame enthusiasts that are all committed to designing, developing, and running high quality 20+ person games. We currently are devoted to developing this genre and bringing these games to everyone! Based in NYC, we work with groups across the US, Canada and the world to help them setup their own local Megagame groups and run games of their own.
